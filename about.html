The Story

The Frontier Awaits

Earth's resources are spent. The asteroid belt holds the last known deposits of Voidstone — a mineral worth more than gold, platinum, and titanium combined. Thousands of miners flood the frontier. Some trade. Some fight. Most never return.

Choose a ship, pick a callsign, and launch into a persistent multiplayer world powered by SpacetimeDB. Mine asteroids, sell at Earth's shield station, upgrade your rig, hire wingmen, and decide: push deeper for richer ore, or play it safe?

"The deeper you go, the richer the ore — and the more dangerous the competition. Greed is your greatest enemy."

Risk & Reward

Danger Zones

The world radiates outward from Earth. Each zone is more dangerous — and more profitable — than the last.

🛡 SAFE ZONE

Earth's shield dome. Sell ore, buy upgrades, hire wingmen. No combat allowed. Your haven.

🟢 GREEN ZONE

Beginner fields. Iron and Copper. Low risk, low reward. Good for learning the ropes.

🟡 YELLOW ZONE

Silver and Gold territory. NPCs get aggressive. Other miners eye your cargo.

🔴 RED ZONE

Platinum and Titanium. Full PvP. Kill streaks. Bounties. Only the bold survive.

💀 THE VOID

Voidstone — $200 per unit. The rarest ore in the cosmos. Reach it and you're rich. If you make it back alive.

PvP & PvE

Combat System

Every ship has a laser. Every player is a potential threat. The deeper you go, the more violent it gets.

🔫

LASER COMBAT

Point and shoot. Projectiles are synced in real-time across all players via SpacetimeDB. No lag, no faking.

🛡

HULL & SHIELDS

Shields regenerate over time. Hull doesn't. Manage your health carefully — retreating to Earth heals you fully.

🔥

KILL STREAKS

Chain kills for streak bonuses. Your name lights up on the leaderboard. Everyone sees. Everyone hunts.

💀

BOUNTIES

High-value targets get bounties. Take down the top earner and claim their price on your head.

🤖

AI NPCS

30 AI miners compete alongside you — mining, trading, and fighting for the same ore. They're not passive.

⚡

SHIP ABILITIES

Every ship has a unique ability on cooldown. EMP, Cloak, Afterburner, Shield Bash, Tractor Beam, or Rally.

Gameplay

Core Features

🌐

SpacetimeDB Multiplayer

Persistent multiplayer powered by SpacetimeDB. Event-driven sync — no polling. Players, NPCs, and projectiles all live on the server.

⛏️

Deep Mining System

7 ore types across 5 zones. Mine asteroids, manage cargo capacity, sell at Earth. Risk vs reward at every turn.

🚀

6 Unique Ships

From the nimble Corsair to the massive Carrier capital ship. Each with unique stats, shields, and a signature ability.

⚔️

PvP & PvE Combat

Real-time laser combat with server-synced projectiles. Fight players and AI NPCs. Kill streaks and bounties.

🎖️

Wingman System

Carrier pilots hire AI wingmen — Fighter, Shield, and Mining wings. Build your own private fleet.

📈

5 Upgrade Paths

Engine, Laser, Shields, Cargo, Scanner. Multiple tiers each. Customize your build for combat, hauling, or exploration.

🤖

30 AI Miners

Intelligent NPCs mine, trade, fight, and compete for ore. Host-authority AI synced across all players in real-time.

🔊

Procedural Audio

Dynamic soundtrack and SFX generated entirely in-browser using the Web Audio API. No assets to download.

📱

Zero Install

Runs entirely in your browser. Single HTML file + SpacetimeDB backend. No plugins, no downloads. Click and play.
